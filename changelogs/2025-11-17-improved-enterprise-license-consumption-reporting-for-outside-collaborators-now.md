## Article Content (cleaned)
We’ve made a key improvement to the enterprise licensing CSV export to help enterprises better track license consumption of outside collaborators across organizations.


**What’s new?**  

– Outside collaborators are now explicitly associated with the organization(s) they contribute to in the license CSV export.  

– In the CSV’s **member roles** column, outside collaborator entries will now include a new value in the format: `[org]/[repo]`.


**Why this change?**


This improvement makes it easier to determine which outside collaborators are consuming licenses, especially as enterprise owners and billing managers manage budgets across multiple organizations.


To read more, check out our [documentation](https://docs.github.com/en/enterprise-cloud@latest/billing/reference/license-reports#github-enterprise-cloud-license-report) or join the discussion within [GitHub Community](https://github.com/orgs/community/discussions/categories/announcements).
