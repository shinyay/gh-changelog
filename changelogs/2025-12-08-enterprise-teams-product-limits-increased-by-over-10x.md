# Enterprise teams product limits increased by over 10x

## Overview
The enterprise teams public preview now supports the creation and management of larger teams.

## Detailed Explanation
### Overview
- The enterprise teams public preview now supports the creation and management of larger teams.
- The new limits are:
- Up to 2,500 enterprise teams can now be created per enterprise (increased from 100).
- Each enterprise team can now include up to 5,000 users (increased from 500).
- Enterprise teams remain assignable across up to 1,000 organizations per enterprise.
- These increased limits empower enterprises to manage user access, governance, and Copilot licensing at scale.
- Enterprise teams is currently available in public preview for Enterprise Cloud customers and is expected to be a preview feature to Enterprise Server customers in release 3.20.
- To learn more, see our documentation about Enterprise Teams . If you have feedback about enterprise teams, join us on this discussion .
